## §6 — Outreach sequencing strategy

**Don't fire all emails at once.** Investor diligence is reputation-driven; a coordinated outreach signals desperation. Sequence:

**Week 1:** Send to **Shield Capital + Lux Capital** (top 2 fit). Wait 1 week for response or polite pass before broadening.

**Week 2:** If positive engagement from Week 1, slow down — give those processes time to develop. If polite pass or no response, add **a16z American Dynamism + 8VC + Founders Fund** to outreach.

**Week 3-4:** Tier 2 (Bain Capital Ventures, Felicis, CRV) + Tier 1 stragglers.

**Week 5-8:** Strategic capital (Lockheed Martin Ventures, Booz Allen Ventures) if early-tier conversations are stalling.

**Defer In-Q-Tel** until after Phase II validates (M18+). They almost certainly pass at seed stage; pitching too early burns the relationship.

## §8 — What to expect from each round

**Round 1 — 30-min first meeting:** Pitch + Q&A. Outcome: either follow-on diligence call scheduled OR polite pass.

**Round 2 — 60-min technical diligence call:** Walk through code, architecture, plan. Often joined by a technical partner. Outcome: term sheet conversation OR pass.

**Round 3 — Term sheet:** Valuation, board structure, liquidation preference, option pool, vesting acceleration. Engage outside counsel before signing.

**Round 4 — Diligence + close:** Background checks, IP assignment review, employment-agreement review. 4-8 weeks to wire.

**Typical seed timeline from first meeting to wire:** 8-16 weeks.

**Expect:** 80%+ first-meeting passes. The 20% that progress are the real pipeline. Plan to take 20-30 first meetings to get 4-6 second meetings to get 1-2 term sheets.
